Learn to prepare this delicius and healthy pancakes with oatmeal, easy and fast!

Description

this recipe can help you to stay in a good form and have a healthy life, because the oatmeal generate a good level of satiety,and is good for the cardiovascular system. the eggs are a good way to get protein, just as milk

Ingredients

    you need the following ingredients:
  • Milk
  • Oatmeal
  • Eggs
  • oil or butter (to don't glue the pancakes)

Steps to do this recipe

To do this recipe you next follow the next steps

  1. Put the oatmeal in a bowl
  2. Mix with a quarter cup with Milk
  3. Add the eggs and continue mixing
  4. Finally, put the mix in a hottest pan
  5. Enjoy and good profit
